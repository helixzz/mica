# Mica (觅采) — Brand Design System

## Purpose
Internal procurement management system for IT departments (<100 employees, <300 orders/month).

## Colors

```
Primary:    #8B5E3C  (Otter Brown)
PrimaryBg:  #F8F4F1  (Warm Cream)
Dark:       #1A1512  (Deep Espresso)
Light:      #FFFFFF  (White)
Accent:     #C67A3D  (Amber Gold)
Success:    #4CAF50  (Green — for completed/approved states)
Muted:      #9E8E82  (Warm Gray — for secondary text)
```

## Typography
- Headlines: Inter, 700 weight, -0.02em spacing
- Body: Inter, 400-500 weight
- Mono: JetBrains Mono (for data/numbers)

## Corners
- Cards: 8px
- Buttons: 6px
- Modals: 12px

## Tone
Professional yet approachable. Efficiency-first. Clean, warm, modern.
Not cold/corporate. Not playful/childish. Confident and helpful.

## Logo
Otter mascot illustration — circular, 32px, used in header and favicon.

## Motion
Smooth, purposeful. Not bouncy or aggressive. Ease: power3.out for entrances, power2.in for exits.
