# FAQ

## I cannot find a record I created

Use global search first. If it still does not appear, check whether your role or department scope limits what you can see.

## Why can I view a page but not edit it?

Mica uses role-level, field-level, and row-level permissions. Some actions are restricted even if the page itself is visible.

## Why is a payment not available yet?

Check whether the related contract, payment schedule, invoice, or approval state has reached the required step.

## I use SSO. Can I still have a local account?

Yes. Depending on the administrator’s setup, local and SSO identities can be linked to the same user record.

## Where should administrators go for deployment or SSO setup?

Start from [Admin Guide Overview](admin/00-overview.md).
